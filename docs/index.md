## Welcome to Stock Prediction

The Stock Prediction web app is a Django web app where users can track stock market prices and receive esimated prices based off of a TensorFlow Neural Network. [READ MORE](https://github.com/SCCapstone/StockPrediction/wiki/Project-Description)

### About Our Web Application 
The Stock Prection Web Application is an application built through Django Rest Framework, React.js, and a collection of APIs and Neural Networks that allow you to predict the prices of your favorite stocks in the stock market.

### How to Use Our Web Application 
To begin using our application, simply search a stock by its ticker or name, and hit enter!
<img width="503" alt="Screen Shot 2021-04-22 at 10 12 13 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65428832/115729365-5ba3b300-a353-11eb-81a9-808eebcce8c2.png">

Once your stock has loaded, you have the option to add it to your watchlist. After adding it to your watchlist,
<img width="624" alt="Screen Shot 2021-04-22 at 10 12 31 AM" src="https://user-images.githubusercontent.com/65428832/115729385-5fcfd080-a353-11eb-8099-d263f6492f10.png">

you can either have the price predicted, which will give you a 30 day prediction of the stock, or you can remove the stock if you are no longer interested in it.
