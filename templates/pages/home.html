Welcome to Stock Prediction Home Page

Welcome to the Stock Predictor home page, where you are able to predict the

prices of your favorite stocks in the stock market. To begin, simply search a stock ticker, and hit enter!

Once your stock has loaded, you have the option to add it to your watchlist. After adding it to your watchlist,

you can either have the price predicted, which will give you a 30 day forecast prediction,

or you can removce the stock if you are no longer interested.
